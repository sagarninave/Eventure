Chrysalis

Chrysalis is an event specially organized by MCA department, which mostly lasts for 4-5 days. The meaning of Chrysalis is “When a baby butterfly or a moth turned itself into an adult stage”. Similarly we want our students to think adult and be ready for their upcoming challenges. Chrysalis event does take care of every individual’s extra curricular intelligence and give the students a chance to putforth their creativity. We have Student Representative Council (SRC) ,consisting of First, Second and Final year students. Department gives SRC members the authority to gracefully manage this event which shows their leadership, managerial and communication skills. One of the subevent also shows their technical skills and cultural event brighten the student’s dancing and singing performance. MCA department staffs and students together celebrate this event and make the event a grand success.
